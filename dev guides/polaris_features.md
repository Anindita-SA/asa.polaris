# Polaris Master Features Catalog & Curriculum Roadmap

Polaris is a **Premium Life Guide & Self-Evolution Dashboard** engineered specifically to merge spatial alignment with tactical, ADHD-friendly daily execution. This catalog documents all distinct, high-end features and details the current vs. projected state of the Personal Curriculum.

---

## 1. The Personal Curriculum (Current vs. Projected)

The Curriculum is your structured self-education hub. It is where you organize what you are studying or consuming to drive your core identity nodes.

### ── Current State (The Foundation)
1. **Academic/Career/Self Partition:** Chapters and topics are partitioned under three master node tabs:
   * **Career:** Covers hardware design (KiCad PCB capture), portfolio writing, and MSc application tracking.
   * **Academic:** Covers DAB converter modulation strategies and your NIT Trichy semester coursework.
   * **Self:** Tracks progressive overload parameters (Greek Commoner workout) and Substack writing schedules.
2. **Standard Task Lists:** Topics function as flat checkable lists within an expandable chapter container.
3. **Completion Progress:** Complete topics award standard XP and display a basic numeric fraction progress indicator (e.g., `4/6`).

### ── Projected State (The Life Navigator Overhaul 🚀)
1. **The Media & Watchlist Integration:** A dedicated golden **Media & Reading** tab is added. It turns your curriculum into a personal Letterboxd/Goodreads:
   * Seeded with three default chapters: `Books 📖`, `Movies 🎬`, and `Shows 📺`.
   * Integrates an entry form to log **Target/Completed Dates**, custom **Personal Context Notes** (e.g., *"Recommended by Vignesh"*), and interactive **Star Ratings (`⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐`)** saved under the `description` field.
2. **Drag-and-Drop Reordering:** Allows you to fluidly drag chapters and topics to rearrange your learning path as your curiosity shifts.
3. **Curriculum-Goal Integration:** Allows you to click any curriculum chapter or topic and directly convert it into an active Daily/Weekly goal on your Goals Board with a single tap.

## 2. Definitive Features Catalog

The following is the complete inventory of distinct premium features currently designed for implementation:

### Component 1: Endless Leveling Engine & 200-Level Curve
* **Gently Scaling Compact Quadratic Formula:** Scales using $\text{XP} = 15 \times L^2$ (Level 1 to 200, capping at a highly realistic 600,000 XP).
* **20 Dynamic Rank Titles:** Spans 20 titles (from Stargazer up to Ascended Visionary) with Grade increments (e.g. `Circuit Weaver, Grade 8`).

### Component 2: Daily Repeating Task Loop
* **Togglable Recurring Icon:** A loop icon in the Journal tasks allows marking tasks as `recurring`.
* **Auto-Cloning Forward:** Automatically carries active previous repeating tasks forward as fresh, unchecked items when a new day loads.

### Component 3: Campaign Goals vs. Side Quests
* **The Main Campaign:** Hierarchical, parent-linked goals representing your core academic/career trajectory to guarantee alignment.
* **Side Quests Guild:** Standalone curiosity-driven targets (sketching, languages, Bangladesh cooking, fiction) with unique **Compass Badges** and soul-feeding rewards.

### Component 4: Spatial Drill-Down Sync
* **Camera Focus:** Clicking any subnode star on your spatial Constellation Map triggers a visual zoom-in transition and filters your **Goals** and **Timeline** lists below to show only items linked to that star.
* **Camera Focus Reset:** Clicking anywhere on empty space resets the zoom and displays all goals.

### Component 5: "Whispering" Context Layers
* **Minimalist Card Faces:** Keeps card faces clean—showing only title, progress, and deadline.
* **Expandable Drawer:** Clicking a subtle "Clarity Anchor" (Info icon) expands a glassmorphic accordion revealing your personal **Why-Now Context, Guiding Quotes, or Creative Descriptions**.

### Component 6: Seamless Goal Editing
* **Pen (Edit) Icon:** Replaces rigid add/delete mechanics with an elegant modal to update Goal Title, Target Metrics, Units, Deadlines, Parent Links, and XP Rewards on the fly.

### Component 7: Unified Goal-Timeline Map
* **Dated Roadmap:** Compiles master milestones together with dated Goals chronologically on the **Timeline** panel, rendering a single, glowing flight path.

### Component 8: Fixed AI Timeline Step Generator
* **Groq JSON Schema Fix:** Corrects the JSON Mode conflict by explicitly requesting a valid JSON object with a `"steps"` string array key, guaranteeing 100% successful, ADHD-friendly micro-step generations.

### Component 9: AI Goal Alignment Auditor
* **Star-Map Hybrid Algorithm:** Invokes Groq using SMART (for short-term) and HARD (for long-term) models to deliver heartfelt validation followed by scannable, constructive critique.

### Component 10: Google Calendar Sync & Workload Estimator
* **Sync Button:** Clicking sync next to daily tasks opens a pre-populated Google Calendar Quick Add URL pre-filled with titles, estimated durations, and parents.

### Component 11: Integrated Pomodoro Focus Soundtracks
* **No Screen Clutter:** Standing Music Player is deleted to reclaim real estate.
* **Footer Audio Deck:** Elegant ambient track selections (Lofi, White Noise, Rain) and Play/Pause buttons embedded directly inside the Pomodoro widget footer with auto-pause on timer completion.

### Component 12: Tactile I/O System (Ground-Up Overhaul)
* **Accrued Logging:** Real-time session calculations and a tactile "Log Accrued Minutes" button so early stops/pauses still log properly.
* **Sliding Pill Toggle:** Highly tactile sliding toggle with glowing borders (Amber for Input, Emerald for Output).
